OUR STORY


IN CRUST WE TRUST!
Terre del Sapore (Land of Flavor in Italian) is a real Italian Neapolitan Pizzeria and Restaurant in the heart of Coral Gables. Under new management since January 2019, and owned by Italians, “Sapore” is proud to use the best ingredients and keeping the Italian traditions alive. Our secret is the dough. After numerous attempts to find the perfect balance between maturation, fermentation and flour blend, “Sapore” has found the best pizza dough you will ever eat.
